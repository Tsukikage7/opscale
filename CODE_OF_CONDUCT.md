# Code of Conduct

Opscale follows a simple contributor standard:

- Be respectful and direct.
- Discuss technical tradeoffs with evidence.
- Do not harass, threaten, or discriminate.
- Do not share credentials, private database output, or personal data in issues or pull requests.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate these rules.
Repeated or severe violations can result in blocked participation.
